Learn Ashtanga Yoga:

Ashtanga Yoga is one of the oldest Vinyasa yoga series that originated in the early 20th century by K. Pattabhi Jois in Mysore, India. If you have already mastered Hatha Yoga or Vinyasa Yoga, this is a good one to try. Ashtanga requires discipline and strength, however, it meets each person where they are, which makes the journey a learning curve but not intimidating.
