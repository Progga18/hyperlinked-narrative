March 2nd, 2026

Common Myths:

Line.

Myth 1: You have to practice it first thing in the morning.

Practice when time permits.

Myth 2:Ashtanga is only for the young and flexible.

Anyone can practice with personalized modification.

Myth 3:Ashtangis Shouldn't do other forms of movements.

Not true! Many practitioners do other form of movement alongside. Such as, strength training, walking, pilates, and yin yoga.

Myth 4: You must do your full ashtanga practice everyday to benefit.

Yoga should support your schedule. If time doesn't permit, you can choose to do the half primary series or just do the two Sun Salutations and just the standing/sitting/finishing sequence. Which ever works best for you.

Myth 5: You must practice 6 days a week to benefit.

Practice how often your lifestyle permits. Consistency over perfection!
